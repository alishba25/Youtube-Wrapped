Getting your watch-history.json

  1. Go to takeout.google.com while signed in to the account you watch YouTube with
  2. Click "Deselect all", then check only YouTube and YouTube Music
  3. Click "All YouTube data included" → keep only history checked
  4. Set the export format for history to JSON (not HTML)
  5. Create the export, wait for Google's email, download and unzip it
  6. Find watch-history.json inside the "history" folder and upload it here
